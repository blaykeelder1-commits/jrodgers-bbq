POLICY — CROSS-CONTAMINATION

Video 3
Policy
CROSS-CONTAMINATION
PREVENTION
Raw meat never touches ready-to-eat food.
Raw meat on the bottom shelf — always.
Separate cutting boards. Change gloves between tasks.

POLICY — FIFO

Video 3
Policy
FIFO: FIRST IN, FIRST OUT
Every item gets labeled with the date it was prepped or received.
Use the oldest product first. New product goes behind old.
Undated product is a food safety violation.

POLICY — SICK

Video 3
Policy
SICK POLICY
Vomiting, diarrhea, fever, or foodborne illness symptoms:
Stay home and notify your manager.
Coming to work sick in a kitchen is not dedication — it's a health hazard.

THE SWEET SPOT — 225-250

Video 3
The Sweet Spot
225-250°F
Below 225: not cooking efficiently, risk the danger zone.
Above 250: rushing the smoke, losing flavor and tenderness.
Monitor throughout the cook. Don't set it and walk away.
