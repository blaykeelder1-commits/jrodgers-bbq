THE HALF-FULL RULE

Video 2
Policy
THE HALF-FULL RULE
No pan should ever drop below half full during service.
When you see it getting low, communicate with the kitchen immediately.
Make rounds every 15 minutes to stir the buffet so it looks fresh.
If a crust looks dull, flaky, or dried out — request the kitchen
come out and flip the item into a clean pan.
