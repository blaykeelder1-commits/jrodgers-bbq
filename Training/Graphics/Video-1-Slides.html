POLICY — ATTENDANCE

Video 1
Policy
ATTENDANCE &
PUNCTUALITY
On time means early. If you need to call out, contact your manager
as far in advance as possible. This is a small team —
when one person doesn't show up, everyone feels it.

POLICY — DRESS CODE

Video 1
Policy
DRESS CODE &
HYGIENE
Your uniform should be clean, pressed, and complete every shift.
Clean hands, clean nails, hair pulled back or covered.
Personal hygiene is non-negotiable in food service.

POLICY — PHONE

Video 1
Policy
PHONE POLICY
Your phone goes away when you clock in.
Customers should never see you on your phone.
Your attention belongs to the people in this restaurant.

POLICY — ATTITUDE

Video 1
Policy
ATTITUDE & CONDUCT
No gossiping in front of customers. No arguing where anyone can hear.
Drama stays outside. When you walk through that door,
our customers deserve your full, positive energy.
